This is about, what else, this site

Call me Roop. Some years ago - never mind how long precisely - I created a blogger account. (Sorry, Moby Dick.) And I've been a very reluctant and occasional blogger ever since, but a blogger nevertheless. And, per how things normally evolve from there, here is my site. Behold and, er, just behold it, ok?
